Soft Skills

In the last two years I had several opportunities to improve my soft skills.

Election comitee

Role:
Responsible for PR, communication and advertisement
Description:
As a part of the election comitee of the FDP Schaffhausen our general task was to ensure the success in the cantonal elections in Schaffhausen in 2020. Our comitee held monthly meetings, where we kept track of the progress in each area of responsibility. I was tasked to establish an internal communication stream. Together with other members of the comitee we developed a process where we integrated Slack and Dropbox to enable communications across areas of responsibility. Furthermore, I took over part of the administrations tasks and modernised the partys website, while also developing a process to audit and publish content for the website.

ETH innovation project

Role:
Responsible for web infrastructure and integration
Description:
As part of the curriculum we had to find a solution as a group of 5 students to combat any problem we had because of the corona virus. Our group decided to build a website which took the latest data from the zurich office for statistics to calculate ones risk to get infected and the impact one had on the spread of the virus. During this project I gained experience in working in an area outside of our expertise with others as a team. We successfully finished the project on time and won the second place in the innovation award of the ETH Product Development Group.

Military squad leader

Role:

Description:
During my military service as a reconnaissance scout I had the opportunity to learn how to effectively lead a group of up to 8 people under high pressure. I consistently had to prepare and hold lessons for my soldiers, give presentations to superiors and lead my group during exercises. Furthermore, I completed the following modules from the "Swiss Association for Leadership Training": Team leadership, conflict management, self management, verbal communication, written communication and presentations.
